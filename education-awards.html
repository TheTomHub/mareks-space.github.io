Education

High-quality technical education abroad has been my aim for a long time, which is why I left my home country after graduation, according to rankings, at best tech high school in Slovakia.

Followed by application for the Ivy League universities. The University of Cambridge, according to rankings 4th best university in the world, invited me for interviews where I exceeded their expectations in Computer Science but didn't persuade them with my math skills.

I get the offer from University College London, according to rankings 7th best university in the world, invited me for competitive Information Management for Business programme. I always believed in the importance of intersection between humanities and computing as science what led me to gain deeper software understanding as an undergraduate.

Continuing with studying business would allow me to contribute to a better world in the future, either as a developer, IT manager, or an entrepreneur.
